About Us
With years of industry experience bringing dynamic shows, we have a proven record of success through creativity and vision, having produced and commissioned over 150 hours of high-quality entertainment television.
All Is Media - Aim is a production company that aims to produce capturing scripted and unscripted television series, shows, events and specials along with content distribution and studio consultancy. Our agile, lean team has a wealth of experience in content production with longstanding relationships with regional networks and international distributors. Our aim is to create dynamic content and deliver groundbreaking, diverse stories to a multicultural Arab world that travels globally..
In addition to international format adoption, original production our business model is built on creating mutually beneficial, sustainable partnerships with leading local and international companies to deliver content that’s next to none!
Sustainable Productions. As a huge amount of environmental pollution and waste lies behind productions, one key strategy for Aim – All Is Media® is to create a conscious effort to go green on set with all its production.
Team
Endowed with a wealth of experience and extensive knowledge in creating capturing content , our team is handpicked from the best international and regional creators. All Is Media - Aim was founded by Mai El Khalifa, who for more than two decades has worked with all the top major broadcasters in the Arab world. Mai’s expertise has spanned through all areas of content production right through to acquisition, commissioning international formats and supervising independents on content.
